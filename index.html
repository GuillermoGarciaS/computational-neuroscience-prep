The Boundary

These models do not explain consciousness. They map where structure appears—and where it ends.

Working thesis

A signal becomes representational only relative to a system that can extract stable structure from it and use that structure for classification, prediction, or transformation. Entropy helps describe uncertainty, but it does not by itself explain meaning or experience.

Boundary condition: detection can produce usable structure without proving that the system understands or experiences anything.
