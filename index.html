From Entropy to Experience

A long-term project of computational experiments on information, uncertainty, representation, and the limits of explaining subjective experience.

Experiments

Experiment 1 — File Size, Structure, and Predictability

How structure affects predictability and why repetition is not meaning.

Experiment 2 — Entropy, Content, and Representation

Same length does not mean same information. Distribution matters.

Experiment 3 — Signals Over Time

A study of how entropy depends on observation scale, and how structure appears or disappears depending on the window used.

Experiment 4 — Detectability of Structure

When patterns are actually detectable—and when entropy alone fails to capture them.

Experiment 5 — Constraints and Output

How constraints shape structure and limit possible outputs.

Projects

Project 1 — Signal Transformation Systems

From signals to structured outputs using simple layered systems.

Project 2 — Multi-Channel Integration

How multiple inputs interact, combine, and transform uncertainty.
